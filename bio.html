About Me

Hi, I am Tony Sargent, I am based in Sydney, Australia. I have spent over 20 years in hardware engineering and solution development
typically, in high end embedded and government applications. I have been lucky enough to see this digital revolution occur, watching
everything become smaller, quicker and mobile on an unfathomable scale. Also observing the freedom and open accessibility of data formats being more usable.
Now with more reliance on human machine interface, re-ignited my interest in the ever-evolving software environment.
Following on from an original qualification in 2003, this course offered by MIT xPro on MERN stack and Java script, has helped me to look out-side the box, at the way we interact with each other and machines.
